Patents

  • A device for making garlands (Patent Pending) : The semi-automatic foot-operated flower garland knotting and packaging system consists of a knotting device operated by a foot pedal. The user needs to press the pedal with his feet and keep the flower against the thread and the flower garland will be knotted! This proves very easy for those who may have some problems in their hands and may find it difficult and painful to make flower garlands.
  • Protective Device With Realtime Impact Monitoring And Method Thereof : (Patent Pending) The present invention relates to a protective device. More particularly, the present invention relates to a protective device, which alerts the user with real-time impact monitoring, and probable injuries with pre-registered members and surroundings.
  • Device and Method to Estimate the Wage : (Patent Pending) The idea of a device, which can calculate the exact amount of money to be paid to porter based on time put in, distance travelled and load carried. The innovators always observed porters arguing with customers about what the amount needed to be paid to them and hence thought of a device that can benefit both porters and travelers.
